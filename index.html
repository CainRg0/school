Story

The Strange Noise in the Night

Hotel Elegance had always been a place of luxury and elegance. Its polished mahogany floors and crystal chandeliers spoke of old-world charm, while the whispered tales of eerie happenings on the fifth floor gave the hotel an air of mystery.

Arrival of the Detective

Detective Muriello Pasquale was called to the hotel after the hotel manager, Marco Aprea reported the disappearing of the guest of room 502.

The Witnesses

Di Mauro Giuseppe, the Tourist, claimed he saw a shadow near the door to Room 502 around 11:50 PM, just before the noise. Davide Scognamiglio, the Waiter, delivered drinks in the room 502at 11:30 PM. he stated that the guest looked really anxious. Giuseppe Russo, the Security Guard, confirmed that the fire escape ladder had been used.

The Hidden Clues

Muriello found several important clues: A flipped table, a broken vase and footprints near the window.

Uncovering the Truth

The guest in Room 502 arranged a meeting with someone they trusted. He looked anxious. The meeting went wrong when a mysterious man in black, entered the room. As soon as the guest saw the man he started panicking. In the bat of an eye, the man knocked out the guest and then proceed to knock him out. He then took the body and escaped from the fire escape ladder. The mystery remained unsolved.
