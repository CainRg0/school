The Strange Noise in the Night

Hotel Elegance had always been a place of luxury and elegance. Its polished mahogany floors and crystal chandeliers spoke of old-world charm, while the whispered tales of eerie happenings on the fifth floor gave the hotel an air of mystery.

Arrival of the Detective

Detective Muriello Pasquale had been staying at the hotel, seeking peace away from the chaotic world of crime-solving. But old habits die hard. The moment he heard the commotion, he stepped into the corridor, ready to take charge.

The Witnesses

Di Mauro Giuseppe, the Tourist, claimed he saw a shadow near the door to Room 502 around 11:50 PM, just before the noise. Sam Thompson, the Waiter, delivered drinks at 11:30 PM. Marcus Russo, the Security Guard, confirmed that the fire escape ladder had been used.
